Investment Consultation and Management

Investment Principles

We maintain strict investment criteria, and many stars must align for us to allocate client capital. We will not invest unless we understand the business, its competitors, and industry well. Generally we prefer businesses with a durable economic advantage, strong management, and a high return on equity with manageable debt. We study many companies but choose to own only our best ideas when offered reasonable prices, and will turn down far more opportunities than we select. We seek companies where managers are shareholders and act like owners.

Disciplined Exection

We only purchase securities we believe to be trading well below their intrinsic value, and will opportunistically exit investments when offered a price well beyond their current or near-term future value. We may also exit positions early if a more attractive opportunity arises. When no companies meet our investment criteria, we will hold cash until one inevitably does. Our highest priority is the preservation of our clients' capital.

Long Term View

We build all investment positions with a margin of safety; we will ignore short term price gyrations, and instead follow the development of the underlying fundamentals. When a company with an excellent management culture continues to perform well and remains fairly valued, we will remain proud owners and allow them to compound for many years.
